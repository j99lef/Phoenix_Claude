1. Introduction

TravelAiGent ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information.

2. Data Controller

Email: privacy@travelaigent.com
Address: [Your Company Address]

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted), name, phone number
  • Travel Preferences: Home airports, preferred airlines, dietary restrictions, travel style, accommodation preferences
  • Travel Data: Travel briefs, destinations, dates, budget, group members
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, login times
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, features used, searches performed

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract: To provide our travel search and planning services
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional features
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and ensure security
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide personalized travel search and recommendations
  • Manage your account and travel groups
  • Send service notifications about your travel briefs
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure the security of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Amadeus (flight data), OpenAI (AI analysis), AWS (hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for:

  • Account Data: As long as your account is active
  • Travel Briefs: 2 years after last activity
  • Technical Logs: 90 days

You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

8. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@travelaigent.com or use the tools in your account settings.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of passwords using Argon2
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage on AWS infrastructure

10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EEA. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses with service providers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
